Understanding The Suicide Rates in Asia (2012 - 2014)

Choropleth Map Showing The Suicide Rates in Asia

This choropleth map visualizes the severity of the crude suicide rates in Asia from 2012 to 2014. The severity of the crude suicide rates are shown using the red colour luminance. The darker the shade of red, the higher the crude suicide rate in the particular country. and vice versa. From the choropleth map, we know that Japan, Kazakstan, Korea and Mongolia are the top 4 countries with the highest crude suicide rates in Asia from 2012 to 2014.




Line Chart Showing The Trend of The Suicide Rates in Asia

This line chart is a substitute visualization to the choropleth map from above. The line chart provides a better visualization to understand the trend of the suicide rates in Asia from 2012 to 2014.
